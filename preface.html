Royal Global University (RGU), one of the premium universities in India, has continuously acted as a leadership platform that aligns interests of industries, entrepreneurs, and the youth since its establishment in 2017. Aimed at creating and sustaining an environment wherein employment and entrepreneurship can flourish with collaborative efforts amongst an extensive network of influential corporates, individual members, and academic institutes, Royal Global University has been able to provide necessary resources, knowledge, and support to create effective employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.
Standing strong on its deep-rooted virtues of excellence on National Highway 37, Royal Global University, has a campus spread over more than 30 acres of land with centrally air-conditioned areas available for running various courses by experienced faculty in world class infrastructure complimented by all necessary facilities. Students are offered a unique educational experience that prepares the next generation of global citizens to lead and carve a niche for themselves in the world. With its talented and motivated student body and accomplished faculty, R.G.U. is a leading educational hub in the Northeastern region of India that maintains commitment to a variety of exceptional undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs along with additional courses like Behavioural Science and Communicative English, Sports, Cultural Activities, guaranteeing holistic development. Royal Global University ensures that its students-the Royalites are moulded into well-rounded, industry- ready individuals, who are equipped to take on leadership responsibilities by a interconnected network of students and faculty members working very closely with students to solve major scientific, technological and societal challenges.
